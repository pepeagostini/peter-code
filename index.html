Spaghetti Bolognese easy

This is an easy and fast version of the Italian Bolognese sauce. It is a mixture of mixed ground beef with onion and tomato puree over spaghetti. I love this meal because it is super easy to prepare and most people love it.

Ingredients

  • 1 stick (90 grams) of butter
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped 750 grams of ground beef and pork Garlic salt and ground pepper, to taste
    • Heat the butter and oil in a saucepan over medium heat. Sauté the onion for a couple of minutes, add the meat and season with garlic salt and ground pepper. Cook until golden brown and add the tomato puree. Cook over low heat until the meat has cooked and the mash has dried a little.
  • 1 medium can (350 grams) of tomato puree
    • Meanwhile, cook the pasta in a pot with boiling water and salt, until it is al dente, about 10 minutes. Drain and place inside a greased refractory.
  • 1/2 package (250 grams) of spaghetti
    • Pour the Bolognese sauce on top and bake over medium heat for 10 to 15 minutes.
